About Me

I am persuing a Bachelors of Art degree of Studio Art at Lawrence University, Appleton, WI. I was born in San Francisco, CA to a family of artists. Much of my work centers around the themes of memory and grief. I enjoy using mediums that allow for making multiples, such as printmaking, photography, and book arts.
